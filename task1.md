# Task 1: Requirements Elicitation

Our System must figure out before we deliver:

1. Can it dispense the same medication to the same patient more than once within a fixed period?
2. Is there a maximum allowable dose for each medication?
3. Are maximum dose limits defined per dispensing or over a time period?
4. Should we track dispensing per patient?
5. Do controlled substances require stricter limits?
6. What should happen if the system trys to dispense medicine exceeding the allowed dosage?
7. Are patient identifiers unique?
8. Can different patients receive the same medication independently?
9. Is the quantity of medication important or only the dosage per unit?
10.Are there time constraints that must be enforced by the system?
